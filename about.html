Established 2015

A Legacy of Industrial Excellence in Iraq

Founded in the strategic industrial hub of Basra, Fourth Corner has rapidly evolved from a local startup into a premier multi-specialized Iraqi partner. We serve as a critical link in the nation's infrastructure, delivering high-tier engineering, safety, and fabrication solutions that provide the operational backbone for Iraq’s growth.

Our corporate philosophy is rooted in engineering rigor and operational discipline. By seamlessly combining local insight with international safety standards, we provide the Oil & Gas, petrochemical, and energy sectors with the absolute reliability required for high-stakes environments. We don't just provide services; we build the sustainable frameworks for a safer industrial future across the region.
